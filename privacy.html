Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

MyTracker is a personal health and fitness tracker built and maintained by Gonçalo Nespral. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Collect

MyTracker collects the following data that you provide or that is generated through your use of the app:

  • Health and fitness data: food entries, workout logs, and weight entries that you manually record.
  • Activity data from third-party services: if you connect Strava or Google Health, MyTracker imports your activity history from those services.
  • Account information: your email address or GitHub username, provided via GitHub OAuth through Supabase Auth.
  • App settings: preferences, calorie targets, and profile details (age, weight, sex) used for calculations.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the app — to display your health history, calculate nutrition targets, and sync with connected services. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose beyond providing the app's functionality.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a private Supabase database. Row-level security ensures that only you can access your own data. API keys and third-party credentials (Strava, Google Health, Anthropic) are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Integrations

MyTracker optionally integrates with:

  • Strava: to import and push activity data. Governed by Strava's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Health: to import and push activity data. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude: AI-assisted food and workout logging. You provide your own API key; requests are made directly from your browser to Anthropic's API. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

MyTracker only requests the minimum permissions necessary from each service.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual entries at any time within the app. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact open an issue on GitHub.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

MyTracker does not use cookies. It uses browser localStorage to store your theme preference, app settings, and OAuth tokens for connected services. This data never leaves your device except as part of authorized API calls to the respective services.

7. Children's Privacy

MyTracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions or requests regarding your data: open an issue on GitHub
