Activities tab

Calendar view, activity log, streak, and stats — all in one tab.

Layout

The tab is split into two columns. The left has the calendar and stats widgets. The right is the activity log — activities for the selected month in reverse chronological order, with a + Log activity button at the top.

Calendar

A monthly grid (Mon–Sun) showing when you trained. Days with an activity show the activity type icon. Tapping a day scrolls the log to that date. Use ‹ › to go back through previous months.
