Nutrition tab

Calorie heatmap, food log, macro averages, and weight tracking.

Layout

The tab is split into two columns. The left has the nutrition calendar, stats, and weight log. The right is the food log — entries for the selected month in reverse order, with daily calorie and macro totals in each day header.

Nutrition calendar

A monthly grid colour-coded by how close your calorie intake was to your target each day. Full green means within ±25% of target; the colour fades toward grey as you drift further — there's no hard cutoff, just intensity. Each logged day shows the day number and an arrow (↑ surplus · ✓ on target · ↓ deficit). Use ‹ › to navigate months.
